ABOUT SCHIZION

HEALTHY SOUL FOR HAPPY LIFE

SCHIZION stand for Schizophrenia Detection and designed to prevent any late damage causes by it. SCHIZION is a web-based expert system application using php. In this application there are several option and question targeted to schizophrenic and their care giver which relative to detect whether or not their loved one suffering from schizophrenia or not. It also gave some advices and early treatment for patient with schizophrenia

Thus, This application is expected to facilitate the public to detect schizophrenia since early stages. Therefore, patients can get the right treatment in order to reduce the prevalence of schizophrenia.

ADVANTAGES

  • Provides easy access for early detection for schizophrenia
  • Lessen consultation fees from psychiatrists and psychologists
  • Provide as a tool to determine whether a person is schizophrenic or not

GOAL

To reduce the prevalence of schizophrenia in Indonesia by increasing self awareness since early ages

MISION

  • Provides general information about schizophrenia, symptomps and its types.
  • Provides information related to preventive and curative efforts against the development of schizophrenia.
  • Provides early diagnostic from schizophrenia both through patients and other people around them.
  • Provides information related to mental health services available in Indonesia.
  • Latest information on mental health.

SCHIZOPHRENIA

Schizophrenia is a serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally. Schizophrenia may result in some combination of hallucinations, delusions, and extremely disordered thinking and behavior that impairs daily functioning, and can be disabling. People with schizophrenia require lifelong treatment.

DIAGNOSE

The diagnostic feature is a key feature of the schizion application, the purpose of this feature is to provide an initial diagnosis of schizophrenia. in this feature the user is divided into 2: patients and caregiver, on this feature the user will be given questions related to the symptoms of schizophrenia, after the patient or care giver answer all the questions that exist then in the final stage there will be conclusions and solutions

TREATMENT

A treatment feature is a feature that explains the therapy that can be done by the patient schizofreni, therapy contained in treatment feature is categorized into three namely medicine therapy, psichological teraphy and self therapy.
